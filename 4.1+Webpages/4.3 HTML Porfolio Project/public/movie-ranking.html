The Best Movies according to Guste

My top 3 movies of all-time

'Interstellar' by Christopher Nolan

My favourite sci-fi movie: so beautiful to watch, to listen and to feel.
Literally an ocean of tears and emotions.
Thank you Christopher Nolan and Hans Zimmer for such a masterpiece.

'The Lord of the Rings' Trilogy by Peter Jackson (J.R.R Tolkien books)

One of my favourite cinematographic genre is medieval fantasy, for sure.
I cannot choose just one of them, they all are a must to watch movie-pack.
Simply marvellous and amazing!
If you love medieval fantasy, you know for sure what I'm talking about.

'Inception' by Christopher Nolan

Another Nolan's sci-fi masterpiece.
Audiovisually hilarious and really interesting and complex plot that blows my mind!
You can see I really love the type of movies that could break my brain in thousand pieces.
